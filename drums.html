Imagine playing the drums and feeling like you're already in a band, only it's your very first lesson. Here's your chance to make your dream a reality. It's time to really enjoy yourself.

Quite simply, with my approach, I'm your guitarist. That's right! I come to your place with an electric guitar and a (small but loud enough) amp. I teach you how to play and back you up with my strong rhythm. This approach works! In fact it works far better than I ever imagined, and it's ten times as fun. From day one my students are learning what it feels like to jam with another person! As they progress they become far more musical and much less mechanical. These are just a few of the advantages to my unique approach. Yes, I teach rudiments. Yes I can teach you to read drum music.
