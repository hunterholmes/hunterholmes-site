About me, Hunter Holmes!

Hi. I'm Hunter Holmes. I've been teaching guitar in the Newton/Wellesley area for over 20 years. I'm also a Berklee graduate. If your looking for a teacher to come to your home please read on. My lessons are unique. If there's one thing I'm not, it's a" professional page turner." I performed for the President of the United States, Bill Clinton at his inauguration January of 1993. What's more important to you is that I know how to teach and work with people (all ages). Leave it to me, I'll bring out your best. Please give me a call!
